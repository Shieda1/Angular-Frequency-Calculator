What is the angular frequency?
The angular frequency is a scalar measurement of rotation rate. Simply it is the angular displacement per unit time. It is referred to as ω.
How to calculate angular frequency?
To calculate angular frequency you should use the following formula:
ω = 2π/T
where:
ω = angular frequency
T = period
Why use angular frequency?
The angular frequency is used in order to make some dificult calculations easier.
